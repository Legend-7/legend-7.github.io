Process / Timeline

Clear steps to bring your vision to life, from concept to completion.

Discovery Phase

In this initial phase, we explore your project requirements, goals, and objectives to understand what you need and how we can achieve it together.

Project Proposal

We present a detailed proposal outlining the scope, timeline, and cost of the project based on our discovery phase discussions.

Agreement & Contract

Formalize our partnership with a contract that details the agreed-upon terms, deliverables, and payment schedule.

Research & Planning

Conduct in-depth research and create a strategic plan to guide the development process, ensuring all requirements are met.

Wireframing

Develop wireframes to map out the structure and layout of the website or app, providing a visual guide for the design phase.

Design Mockups

Create detailed design mockups based on the wireframes, showing how the final product will look and function.

Client Review

Present the design mockups to you for feedback and revisions, ensuring the design meets your expectations before proceeding.

Development

Begin the development phase, coding and building the website or app according to the approved design and functionality.

Content Integration

Add and format content into the website or app, including text, images, and multimedia elements.

Testing & QA

Conduct thorough testing and quality assurance to identify and fix any issues before launch.

Client Feedback & Revisions

Gather your feedback and make any necessary revisions to ensure the final product meets your needs.

Launch Preparation

Prepare for the official launch by finalizing all details and ensuring everything is ready for public release.

Project Launch

Officially launch the website or app, making it available to users and clients.

Post-Launch Support

Provide ongoing support and maintenance to address any post-launch issues and ensure continued success.
